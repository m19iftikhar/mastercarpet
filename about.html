ABOUT US

A HISTORY OF INNOVATION

IN OUR 40-YEAR HISTORY, WE HAVE NEVER RESTED ON OUR LAURELS. OUR DESIGN PROCESS HAS ALWAYS BEEN EVOLUTIONARY, CONSTANTLY CHANGING WITH THE TIMES. ABBAS CARPETS RECOGNIZED EARLY ON THAT THE MONOTONY OF PERSIAN RUGS AND DESIGNS DEMANDED NOTHING SHORT OF A REVOLUTION IN THE HANDMADE RUG INDUSTRY. THE DYEING AND WEAVING PROCESS TOO WAS IN DIRE NEED OF CHANGE. From the humble beginnings through years of hard work by its present owner has made the company synonymous with some of the finest wool/silk blend hand knotted carpets in the Afghan, Central Asian, Tibetan & Persian tradition. Besides the Pakistani carpets we have stocks of tribal carpets, popularly called the Khyber range collection, Persian rugs & Kilims, Balochi ‘Textiles’ in a diversity of weaves, sizes, materials, quality, etc.

OUR PRESENT SETUP

The flagship show room and ware house is located in posh locality of Clifton, Karachi and comprises arguably the finest collection of hand knotted carpet in the world.
